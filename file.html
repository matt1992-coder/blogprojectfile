1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business

Information technology is used for storing, protecting, processing, securing, transmitting, receiving, and retrieving information , In Worldwide Businesses IT is a crucial element to how companies operate in a modern environment , IT technology allows companies and organisations to expand their operations in business environments. For example, when companies expand they get bigger and look for ways to store information to focus time on other parts of the business , using IT to help store information in databases and build websites to improve marketing and attract customers through the sales of their product.

Information technology allows companies to reduce costs and increase productivity in areas like sales , accounting, marketing, and human resources so they can centralize their resources on other key aspects of the business. Today, most companies store digital versions of documents on servers and storage devices rather than paper based as this saves time and money and is much more efficient so this shows how companies have used IT as a benefit of technology throughout the years.

Another key way IT has transformed relationships between customers and businesses is through the use of Customer relationship management (CRM) these systems record every interaction a company has with a customer, so that a more personal experience is possible. If a customer calls a call center with an issue, the customer service rep will be able to see what the customer has purchased, view customer information, call up the log manual for that issue and effectively respond to the issue.

Lastly the way Information Technology has improved communication for businesses is changing rapidly , email when first released was effective , easily obtainable and widely used and allowed people to message each other for questions and things relating to companies. Modern 21st century has allowed even more tech advancement for communication , Skype is now used for video conference calls and a message based system , Smartphones are also critical and most people have access to apps that can benefit a work environment like Linkedin and Skype. Most People think IT is mainly computers and Mobile Technology but as part of a business , printers and 3D Printers are what they use modernly and is considered IT technology.

1.4 , Describe the risks that might impact digital projects

There are risks that can impact digital projects in serious ways the main one is funding and companies pulling out if they face financial difficulty , executives of companies can also suddenly pull funding if they don't agree with a project that is not going in the right direction.

Poor Money management and miscalculation of costs for supplies are another bad example of how projects can go wrong , for example a company may go to a cheaper supplier and the money could be wasted if it isn't suitable for the organisation.

Next risk is Schedules and these are major important because companies have to meet deadlines and schedules can go wrong through mismanaging diary systems or technology , the risk that activities will take longer than expected , Slippages in schedule typically increase costs and delay the receipt of project benefits, with a possible loss of competitive advantage.

Performance Risk is another issue within a company and this can occur when a specification for a project and a set way of doing it can lead to issues within a group team environment , one person may take over and know more than the others , this can cause someone to dictate the group coursework and make a project fail due to things being completed incorrectly because of one person.

1.7 Describe legal guidelines and constraints that impact digital projects

Legal Guidelines that impact digital projects are very numerous the first most important one is you have to disclose the Identity of your business , this includes Company Name so you can be identified , the registered address and the place of registration.

Other details needed to be included are the postal address and email address , for people who are not technical and cant access computers the company must include a way to be contacted through non electronic means , so it is key the business has this sorted for digital projects.

GDPR regulations are next and majorly affect businesses these came into effect in may 2018 and improves the rights of people who want to access their own data and how it is being processed , it needs to be made clear when signing up to a company through a form or email application.

Companies can make the customers opt in so data is only given through consent and can make it easier for customers to opt out , lastly also have a process for data breaches in worst case scenarios.
